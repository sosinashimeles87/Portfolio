Projects

Child Behavior Observation

I conducted structured observations of children during play and group activities to explore how their emotions influence their behavior. This experience strengthened my empathy, active listening, and patience—key skills for supporting children’s emotional development.

Mental Health Awareness Project

I created and delivered a school presentation on the importance of mental health and emotional well-being. The project aimed to reduce stigma and encourage open conversations about feelings, emphasizing that mental health is just as important as physical health.

Anxiety Management in Children

This project focused on identifying early signs of anxiety in children and evaluating intervention strategies. Through structured observations and parent interviews, I developed approaches to support emotional regulation and reduce stress in daily routines.
